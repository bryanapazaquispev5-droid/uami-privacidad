Privacy Policy

At Uami, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and the security measures we take to protect it.

General Information

The Uami mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") is a tool dedicated to personalized meal and recipe recommendations, meal plan suggestions, and locating nearby supermarkets. This application is operated by its developers to provide AI-driven nutritional guidance and translation of recipes.

By downloading, installing, and using this Application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the Application.

Data We Collect

To provide you with the best user experience and ensure the proper functioning of Uami, we collect the following types of information:

1. Account & Registration Information:
We use Firebase Authentication to manage user registration and logins. When you register, we collect basic contact information such as your email address, username, and secure credentials.
2. User Preferences & App Activity Data:
We store details about your favorite recipes, user reviews, written comments, and dietary filter preferences. This data is saved both locally on your device (SQLite Room Database) and synced to our servers via Firebase Firestore to keep your profile updated across devices.
3. Device Location:
Our Application requests precise and approximate location permissions (GPS and network) via ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION. This information is processed only in real-time on your device to display nearby supermarkets on the Google Maps screen. We do not store your location history on our servers or share it with third parties.
4. Device Connection State:
We monitor your network connectivity using the ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE permission to ensure the application has an active internet connection before calling our remote API services.

Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

Uami relies on trusted third-party technology providers to deliver essential app features:

  • Google Firebase (Auth & Firestore): For secure user registration, profile sync, and storage of recipe reviews/favorites.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): We request the POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission to send push notifications about recipe alerts or food tips. You can opt-out at any time from your device system settings.
  • Google Maps SDK / Geo APIs: Used to display interactive maps and nearby supermarket points of interest. Use of maps is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • AI & Translation Services: We use on-device translation tools and the Gemini AI API to compile localized recipes and provide automated nutritional advice.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for the following business purposes:

  1. Service Personalization: Recommending recipes that align with your dietary filters and taste preferences.
  2. Map Functionality: Pointing out supermarkets and grocery stores closest to your current location.
  3. Social Interaction: Enabling you to publish reviews, ratings, and comments on recipes.
  4. Performance Monitoring: Identifying software bugs and performance bottlenecks to improve app speed.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal data. We implement industry-standard security measures through Google Firebase, which secures database connections with encryption in transit and holds data in highly protected cloud facilities.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Rights & Account Deletion

You have full control over your personal information:

  • Access and Modify: You can view and edit your profile information at any time within the app's Profile screen.
  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account and all associated user data stored in Firebase (including favorites and reviews), you can make a request by emailing our support contact below. We will process and fulfill your request within 7 business days.
